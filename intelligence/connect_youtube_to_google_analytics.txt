Steps to Connect YouTube Channel to Google Analytics:

1. Sign in to your Google Analytics account.
2. Click on the Admin tab.
3. Select the account that contains your YouTube channel.
4. Click on the Property column and select the property that corresponds to your YouTube channel.
5. Click on the Property Settings tab.
6. Scroll down to the bottom of the page and click on the 'Get Tracking ID' button.
7. Copy the tracking ID.
8. Sign in to your YouTube account.
9. Click on your profile picture and select 'YouTube Studio'.
10. Click on the Settings icon.
11. Click on 'Channel' and then 'Advanced settings'.
12. Scroll down to the 'Google Analytics property tracking ID' section and paste the tracking ID.
13. Click on 'Save'.
